The Background

Roughly five years ago the concept for Drive Bar was concocted (pardon the pun) by one Lisa Lauck as an intersection between her love of cars, and her love of mixology. The natural brain-child was Drive Bar, where people could enjoy the shabby chic style that is remniscent of both a working garage, lovingly looked-after classic and modern cars, and a lounge, while sipping some of the most well mixed cocktails this side of the Mississippi. With a splash of southern thrown in for good measure, Drive Bar is sophisticated while being unpretentious, welcoming and unassuming, and a place for all crowds. Toss in the occasional regional fare from Chef Brandon to spice things up, and comfortable couches, and you won't want to leave.
